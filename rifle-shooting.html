About Rifle Shooting

Rifle Shooting is a very popular sport. Most rifle events are shot st distance between 10 meters and 50 meters although a feww do go out to 300 meters. There is a large selection of Rifle events which only require range space out to 25 meters so the discipline is easily accessible and can be shot on indoor ranges.
Classfication rules allow rifles to be fitted with either iron sights, scopes or red dot sights. Targets vary according to the event being shot. Targets range from traditional concentric circle 'bullseys' targets to various styles of disruptive pattern designs.
