About Swimming

Swimming can be generally considered as an individual sport. There are a number of styles in swimming. Among them, the main four styles are 'freestyle', 'breaststroke', 'backstroke' and 'butterfly'. Swimming competitions are held from these main four styles. In addition to that, there are different categories of swimming competitions such as 'individual medley' and 'relay'.

Our club Swimming

In our club, we provide the membership as,

  • Kids Membership(for memmers below 18 years of age)
  • Adult Membership(for memmers above 18 years of age)

We are organizing annual competitions according to different age categories. Those who are interested in participating for the competitions can participate based on their talents. The winners get the oppurtunity to participate for the inter club competitions.
