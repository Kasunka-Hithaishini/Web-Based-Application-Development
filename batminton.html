About Badminton

Badminton can play both single and doubles at their games.Badminton is a mindful and joyful game.Players have to use their best to mark the win.Physical and mental healthiness is import for this sport.Furthermore,players can build-up new friendships also.
The sport called "Badminton" was known from England in Badminton House-Gloucestersgire at 17th century.In 1893,for the first time in the history was organized the first national badminton association.
In 1990,the championships were open for both ladies and gentalements.The most significat point is, apart from having single and double matches for both parties, they got a chance to play together as a mixed doubles.
